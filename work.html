Work Experiences

National University of Singapore, Singapore

Postdoctoral Research Fellow
Mar, 2023 - Present

Currently, I am serving as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, NUS, Singapore. Initially, I was involved in modernising the energy management system of Singapore’s futuristic port, which is being practically implemented in collaboration with Envision Digital. Now, I am working towards developing an intelligent energy management module for Electric Vehicle and Grid cooperation, soon to be implemented by STRIDES for its EV fleet in two new charging stations. These two projects are sponsored by the Energy Market Authority (EMA) of Singapore.

Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, Rupnagar, India

Research Associate
Mar, 2022 – Nov, 2022

As a Research Associate at IIT Ropar, I contributed to the project "Efficient Energy Management System for Smart Residential Networks via Intelligent Mobile Web Services", funded by the Core Research Grant of the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), New Delhi, India. In this position, my key responsibilities included: 1) Developing and deploying low-cost energy meters to monitor the residential loads at the IIT Ropar campus, and 2) Providing real-time energy consumption management suggestions to campus residences subjected to India's inclining-block-rate (IBR) tariff system. Additionally, I supported the principal investigator with tools procurement and project management tasks.

Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, Rupnagar, India

Teaching Assistant
Jan, 2017 – Mar, 2022

I was a Teaching Assistant for the labs and tutorials of courses: 1) Principles of Electrical Engineering (GE-104, GEL-104), 2) Electrical Machines (EEP-203), 3) Power Systems (EE-309), and 4) Simulation and Analysis of Modern Power Systems (EE-513) at Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Ropar. I have also mentored several summer interns and undergraduate and master’s students in their term projects and final dissertations. Apart from courses, I was assigned for lab development, especially procuring the equipment, for Smart Home Energy Management (SHEMS) facility at SYMAR Lab, IIT Ropar.
